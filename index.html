About Me

I grew up in Utah where I met my wife while going to Utah State university. It was there that I was first introduced to programming while studying Electrical Engineering. I then discovered that that was not for me and changed my major to Mechanical Engineering. By the time that I discovered my love for web design and development I had already invested around five years in engineering.

I thought to myself what is the best thing to do, start over or just finish up what I am doing? I then discovered that I could graduate in one more semester if I changed my major to General Studies in Technology yes this is a real major.So that is what I did. I was hesitant at first because who wants to have a major of "General Studies in Technology"? I sure didn 't in my mind I wanted something more useful like Computer Science. But I then discovered that it would take me another three years at the university in order to do that.

Big Decisions

From there I had some soul searching to do, is it better to get a more prestigious degree or is it better to get out in the work force and have three years of experience under my belt. After doing alot of research I decided that it would be best to graduate and get experience.

So from there I started to look for jobs applying for a few and getting turned down. Then something amazing happened ... someone contacted me and said that they thought I could be a good fit doing software development. The company is called eIntern and I took the job with them. This would give me the real world experience that I need while at the same time I can be paid for it.
